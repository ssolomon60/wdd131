Brigham Young University–Idaho

Developing Disciples of Jesus Christ

Brigham Young University–Idaho was founded and is supported and guided by The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

The university does this by:
  1. Building testimonies of the restored gospel of Jesus Christ.
  2. Providing a high-quality education and learning to those who are ready to be leaders in their field.
  3. Serving as many students as possible within resource constraints.
  4. Delivering education that is affordable for students and the Church.
